# Competitor Research – Clode

This document analyzes 10 companies adjacent to Clode across cloud infrastructure,
platform engineering, DevOps automation, and developer tooling.

Each company is evaluated for overlap, differentiation, and DevRel positioning.

---

## 1. NetFabric
- Website: https://netfabric.ai
- Founder: Benjamin Bichsel
- Location: San Francisco, US
- Primary category: Cloud Infrastructure

**What they do:**  
NetFabric automates cloud networking, helping teams provision and manage network infrastructure across environments.

**Target user (ICP):**  
Platform engineers, cloud infrastructure teams

**Key features:**
- Automated network provisioning
- Policy-based cloud networking
- Infrastructure abstraction

**Pricing:** Not publicly listed

**Positioning:** Simplified cloud networking

**Overlap with Clode:**
- Cloud automation
- Infrastructure management

**How Clode is different:**
- Automates the full application lifecycle, not just networking
- Covers deployment, operations, and monitoring

**DevRel angle:**  
Networking automation is only one piece of platform automation — Clode focuses on the entire operational workflow.

**Content hooks:**
- Why networking tools aren’t enough for platform automation
- From infra scripts to intelligent automation
